 Printing Management System

 https://github.com/MenardMBaladjay/Printing-Management-System


1. PROJECT OVERVIEW DESCRIBE THE APPLICATION
- This system is an managing system where it can compute value of total cost, view number of pages printed. 
  The cashier can also update and cancel orders to avoid duplicate of printed papers and makes the process of printing faster and organized.

2. Problem Statement what problem does the client face?
- Client is facing problems if there are a lot of customers 
  who wants to print or xerox papers which cause computation errors,
  orders may lost or duplicated which can cause hassle for client 
  so client wants to avoid those problems.

3. Objectives List System Goals
- To accept printing orders and can compute total cost automatically, 
  allows viewing of orders, and receive orders information.
  
Print Prices
-Black and White   PHP 2.00/page
- Colored          PHP 5.00/page
- Photo Paper      PHP 20.00/page

4. User Primary users of the system.
- Cashier - The one who will handle customer or printing process.
- Client/Student - The one who will order printed papers.
